Project overview

It is a healthy recipe responsive web app for people who follows a strict diet due to their under-lying health conditions such as diabetes, obesity, heart problem etc and who has dietary restrictions.Easy Meal planning with shopping list and plenty of video guided recipes make life lot easier

Design Process

The design process is based on User-centered design (UCD) where user is the center of the design and development process.

Discovery

Competitive Analysis

Analyzed similar web apps Yummly and Forks over Knives. I did the analysis based on their key objectives, Overall strength, Market advantage, SWOT Aanlysis and UX Analysis.

User Research

5 w's for Health Savvy

What

User can choose a diet and then select a guided video recipe categorized under breakfast,lunch,snack and dinner or can directly search a recipe by the name or the ingredient. User can also go for a weekly/monthly diet plan as per their health issue by an expert nutritionist or create by themselves. They can also make a shopping list.

Who

The app is for the people who wants to follow a diet to resolve or prevent their health issues and a monthly/weekly meal planner to follow.

When

The user will be engaging with the product everyday when they plan for a meal. They will also use the app to follow the guided recipe video if they don’t know the how to make the food.

Where

The user will use the app at home when they r preparing the meal through their phone, iPad or computer. At the grocery store through their phone or iPad while buying the ingredients required for the meal.

Why

In one platform the user can search a recipe, make a weekly/monthly customized or expert made diet plan, create a shopping list and buy the ingredients through the app.

User Interview Synthesis

I interviewed three participants to know about their eating habits, If they have any underlying health issues or they follow any specific diets, how often they cook at home and where they look for a recipe. I tried to identify their needs and goals, pain points and frustration.

  • All the participants cook their meals very often and they all follow a healthy diet for their health-related issues.
  • People these days are following a healthy lifestyle and prefer to cook their meal at home.
  • Noteworthy quote(s) “ I want to eat healthy food but it has to be tasty”.
  • Frustration - The recipes do not mention the nutritional value of the ingredients and the calorie intake.
  • Needs and goals - They all are having some health-related issues and finding recipes with nutritional value is important for them.

MVP : Minimum Viable Product

MVP Objective

This app will allow the users to create a personalized recipe recommendations by choosing a healthy diet and filtering out their dietary restriction or dislikes. They can also directly search a recipe by the name or the ingredient or by cuisine from a wide range of recipes. User can also go for a weekly/monthly diet plan as per their health. They can also make a shopping list.

Goal

The main goal of this Minimum Viable Product (MVP) document is to gain validation from target users that such an app will be used to to make a meal plan or find recipes beneficial for their health-related issue.

User Testing

Early Usability Testing

I conducted usability testing to get a better sense of how I could improve designs for my app. I did few interviews in person and few over skype.I gave them few scenarios in which they were required to take certain actions. I evaluated the ability or inability of users to complete specific tasks and watched, listened and took notes.

I gave them two tasks to perform.

  • Open the app and personalize the recipes according to your likes and dislikes.
  • Create a Meal plan and add recipes to the meal plan.

MoodBoard

After looking at few sources of inspirations i created moodboard to get a more concrete visual direction for my app.

Orange enhances a feeling of vitality and happiness. it draws attention and shows movement but is not as overpowering. Citrus orange is associated with healthy food. It stimulates appetite and is warm, vibrant and flamboyant.

Green symbolizes health, new beginnings and wealth. Green is the easiest on the eyes and can be used to relax and create balance in a design. Green usually used for eco-friendliness, natural, organic, healthy food choices. For those who are conscious about healthy eating, this color is appealing.

Retrospective

My primary objective to design Health Savvy was for people who has underlying health conditions such as diabetics or high blood pressure or obesity or any other diet restrictions and looking for all varieties of healthy recipes which are low on calories, low on carbs, low on sugar, low on fat.

I designed the app in a way that offers personalized guidance in every step with great customized meal plan option and shopping list which improve life in kitchen.

The process of designing this app helped me to understand various UX concepts and how to incorporate humane-centered approach in my design.

Project Overview

Pricepixel is a responsive web app and mobile application for people who wants to buy their favorite products from their favorite retailers like Macy’s, Nordstrom, Belk and many more at the price they want to spend on them . Price pixel is your place to select your store, browse the product, set a price for it and save in your wishlist. Once the price goes below your set price , you will get a notification and you can order it online. You can check the price history from the period you have added the product to your wishlist.
